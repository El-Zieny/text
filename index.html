About

Car X-Ray A Web-based Platform for Simplified Automotive Knowledge. This website will provides clear structured, interavtive information about various cars parts , including engines belts , filters , spark plugs , ignition colis, and tires.

Why Car X-Ray

We make car knowledge simple and accessible for everyone by explaining all major car systems in a clear, visual, and beginner-friendly way. Our platform breaks down complex automotive concepts into easy explanations supported by clean diagrams and practical examples, helping users truly understand how cars work. With a fast search system and a clean, friendly interface, finding information is quick and effortless. We also focus on increasing user awareness of car maintenance, allowing drivers to recognize issues early and make smarter decisions. To make learning even easier, an AI-powered chatbot is available to instantly answer car-related questions and guide users anytime they need help.
